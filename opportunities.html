Opportunities

How could you get involved in our research, or get us to solve your problems?

Consutancy

At Transport Analytics Research Lab (TARLab), we offer expert consultancy services to help organisations and communities navigate the complexities of transport planning and management. Our team specialises in data-driven transport analytics, providing insights and solutions to optimise traffic flow, reduce emissions, and enhance transport resilience. Whether you need support with strategic planning, modelling, or data analysis, we are here to help you achieve your transport objectives with innovative and sustainable approaches. Contact us today to discuss your project needs and how we can collaborate to create more efficient and resilient transport systems.

Research students

We are looking for motivated students in three forefronts:

1. Autonomous vehicles

2. Electrical vehicles and charging systems

3. Transport network resilience

Talk to us about doing a PhD or Master by research. Limited funding are available.
